OUR SERVICES

Our team at your service for your parisian getaway

Royal Hotel offers quiet and elegant accommodation in the centre of Paris. Blending tradition and modern luxury, it welcomes you in a typical 19th century Parisian building that has been very well preserved over the years. Blending a vibrant Parisian atmosphere and a comfortable home-from-home feeling, each of the fully-equipped 57 rooms presents a unique and stylish interior design. Everything here is designed to make your holiday or business trip run smoothly.

OUR SERVICES

Reception

Our warm and polyglot receptionists welcome you round-the-clock. They will do their utmost to help make your stay in Paris as carefree and relaxing as possible. Do not hesitate to contact them for shows and restaurant bookings, to call a taxi or for general information about Paris.

Breakfast

Our breakfast is served daily from 7 am to 10.30 am, in our restaurant or in your room until 11 am. We offer a continental buffet of quality to delight all palates.
