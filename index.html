EXPERIENCE THE CHARM AND ELEGANCE OF PARIS

Haussmannian hotel combining calm and prestige

The 56 rooms offer exquisite diversity. They are classified in six categories so that every guest will be able to find perfect accommodations. From the Business Single, which offers all the essentials of a Executive room with a view of the Arc de Triomphe, according to the stages, you can have a cup of tea while observing the busy Paris streets. All rooms feature light, ceruse-finished oak furniture in a refined setting that contrasts colors and fine materials.

AN EXCLUSIVE NEIGHBORHOOD

Your room next to the main monuments of Paris

The 8th district of Paris is a symbol of elegance, French culture and refinement where you can discover the best of Paris (restaurants, shops, museums). You will also be right next to all the must-see sights of Paris with the Arc de riomphe and Champs-Elysées only foosteps away and all other monuments (Eiffel Tower, Louvre, Montmartre...) less than 20min away by metro.
